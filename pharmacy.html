Pharmacy Frequently Asked Questions

How do I refill my prescription? expand_more
You can refill your prescription through our mobile app, by calling the pharmacy directly at (555) 0123-4567, or by visiting our walk-in counter with your prescription number.
Do you accept international insurance? expand_more
Yes, we work with most major international insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card and a valid ID for processing at the pharmacy counter.
Are generic medications as effective? expand_more
Absolutely. Generic medications are required to have the same active ingredients, strength, and quality as brand-name drugs. Our pharmacists can help you choose the best value for your budget.
